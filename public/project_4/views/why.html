Why Do This?

Before the invention of satelites the only way to send information from place to place was through physical means.
Of course mail was an easy way to send messages but that took forever. The invention of the telegraph solved that, but the
telegraph couldn't cross the sea the same way it could by land. So, instead of buidling poles to hang the cables from, the
inventors of the past decided to lay the cable under the ocean. Eventully that was upgraded to phone lines, and after that the
internet.
