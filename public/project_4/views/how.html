How Do They Do This?

Similiar to unspooling a thread along the floor a ship simply sails along the sea in as straight a line as possible
while the mass of cable held within the hull is unwound and let to sink to the ocean floor. Some cables are even
burried underneath the sand if that is possible and bennificial with the cable in use.
